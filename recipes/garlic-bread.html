Homemade Garlic Bread Recipe

Description

Garlic bread is a delicious side dish that complements a variety of meals, especially Italian cuisine. This recipe will guide you through making your own homemade garlic bread that's crispy on the outside and soft on the inside, infused with the rich flavors of garlic and butter.

What you‘ll need:

  • Bread;
  • Butter;
  • Garlic;
  • Parsley (Optional);
  • Sprinkles of Parmesan cheese (Optional).
