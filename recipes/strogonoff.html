Strogonoff Recipe

Description

Strogonoff is a popular dish of Russian origin, known for its creamy sauce and tender pieces of meat. This recipe will guide you through making a flavorful strogonoff that's perfect for a comforting meal.

What you‘ll need:

  • Beef strips or chicken pieces;
  • Mushrooms;
  • Onion;
  • Garlic;
  • Sour cream;
  • Tomato paste;
  • Beef or chicken broth;
  • Olive oil or butter;
  • Salt and pepper;
  • Fresh parsley (Optional).
