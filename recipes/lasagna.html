Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat, cheese, and tomato sauce. This recipe will help you create a rich and hearty lasagna that's perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

What you‘ll need:

  • Lasagna noodles;
  • Ground beef or sausage;
  • Tomato sauce;
  • Ricotta cheese;
  • Mozzarella cheese;
  • Parmesan cheese;
  • Onion and garlic;
  • Olive oil;
  • Italian seasoning (Optional);
  • Salt and pepper.
